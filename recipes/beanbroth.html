Bean Broth

A dish that uses up whatever you have in your fridge. Once you master the basic steps of broth making the world will be your soup bowl.

Ingredients

I have not included quantity for any of of the ingredients. That's totally up to you. If you're making this just for yourself then use one carrot, one onion and one celery stalk. If you are cooking for several people, bump up the amount to suit. This is about preference too. Please feel free to add your favourite vegetable. Add pre cooked shredded chicken and omit the Chorizo to the pot 10 minutes before serving to make this a beany chicken soup.

  • Carrot
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Spinach
  • Cabbage
  • Swiss Chard
  • White Bean (or bean of choice)
  • Chorizo (optional)
  • Chilli Flakes (optional)
  • Stock Cube

Steps

  1. Chop vegetables to preferred size. This is a rustic dish so don't worry about precision.
  2. Sweat onions
